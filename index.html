Raising Nigeria's Female
Technology Talents

AboCoders is removing obstacles to a productive and
prosperous life for young women from low income families in
underserved communities in Nigeria.

Who are we?

We are a group of young people
with a keen interest to reduce the
marginalisation and relegation
of young women in Africa by
equipping them with space-age
and relevant technological skills
needed to provide sustainable
livelihoods for them.

What is our goal?

Our goal is to empower over 2500
young women from low-income
communities in Nigeria by the
end of 2023. We aim to achieve
this by conducting relevant
training, and providing access to
further learning opportunities
while also creating employment
opportunities for our target
audience.

Our Focus

Web development

Students under this track are exposed to learning Frontend development using HTML, CSS, Javascript, React and WordPress.

Data science

Students enrolled for this track are trained to be positioned for the evolving interdisciplinary field of Data Science that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from noisy, structured, and unstructured data, and apply knowledge and actionable insights from data across a broad range of application domains

Product design

Despite being a relatively newly introduced track, and presently being offered in only our Kaduna and Minna training centers; we have been able to train over 100 young girls on graphic design, product design and animations under this track.
