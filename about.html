AboCoders is removing obstacles to a productive and
prosperous life for young women from low income
families in underserved communities in Nigeria.
Simply put, for 4 months, we train young women on
coding, digital marketing, hardware, data science,
and graphics so they can use their skills to generate
income and create a better economic situation for
themselves. AboCoders improves the lives of its
beneficiaries by providing secure and sustainable
livelihoods for them through ICT training and
professional mentorship for building sustainable
careers in technology.

Our Community

What happens after our students graduate?

Our students after graduation are inducted into the AboCoders community. This community offers a lifetime membership unless the student decides to cease to be a member. The objective of the community is to provide mentorship, continuous training opportunities, and work/internship opportunities for its members. We also support our alumni with work/learning space in all our centers (academies). These alumni usually grow to become self-sufficient within a time frame of 3-6 months.
